Our Story

At Pizzeria, we don’t just make pizza. We make people happy. Pizzeria was built on the belief that pizza night should be special, and we carry that belief into everything we do. With more than 55 years of experience under our belts, we understand how to best serve our customers through tried and true service principles: We create food we’re proud to serve and deliver it fast, with a smile.

In 1958, two brothers borrowed $600 from their mom to open a pizza place in Wichita, Kansas. They named it Pizzeria, because their sign only had room for eight letters. How profound! Soon, the restaurant grew. Why? The pizza was awesome. The service felt like home. And the customers were treated like family. And we’ve been delivering that same food and service ever since. No one loves pizza more than Pizzeria. That’s why pizza is in our name — and always will be.

Our chefs

There is so much more to being an InspiringChefs than simply knowing the techniques. Many of our chefs began as home-cooks, learning from the best (their mums, grandparents and great-grandparents!) and training at that most-esteemed academy, the culinary school of life. Our chefs are individually chosen because they bring something special to the table. An InspiringChefs needs to have flair, a sense of flavour, a great story, the ability to help you get to know your food as you learn to cook and, most importantly, passion, which will shine through.
